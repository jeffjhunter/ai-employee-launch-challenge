The Challenge Curriculum

This is the step-by-step path that takes someone from curiosity about AI Employees to a much clearer understanding of what role they want, how it should behave, and why they may want help implementing it properly.

1

Choose the role

Pick the kind of AI Employee you actually want to launch and what business problem it should solve first.

2

Shape the personality

Define tone, behavior, scope, and what makes the AI Employee feel distinct instead of generic.

3

Choose the first workflow

Pick one useful thing the AI Employee should do in the real world before trying to make it do everything.

4

Map the launch version

Outline the channel, the memory, the use case, and the assets needed for a practical first version.

5

Position the opportunity

Understand how this becomes a business asset, a client-facing offer, or a role-based service.
